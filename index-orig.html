    Resident Services

    The City offers a wide array of resident services, including assistance with non-emergency issues, municipal services, and support programs.

    Public Documents

    Under state law, every person has a right to access public government records. Use our document search for access. Not all records are public, and you may need to pay a fee to get them.

    Meeting Calendar

    View the schedule of City Council and other public agency meetings. You can search for historical meetings and view the calendar of upcoming meetings, even subscribe to meeting events.

    Commitees & Boards

    View the directory of boards, commisssions & advisory committees. You can search for a specific board and view the current term board members.

    City Open Data

    With Tyler's Open Data Platform, residents, businesses, developers, and community organizations can easily access the most up-to-date data. Our open data platform organizes it in easy-to-understand charts and graphs with context provided through narrative and visualizations, putting your open data program on the path to building strong partnerships for a better community.

    • Transform your data into interactive graphs, charts, and maps
    • Maximize your data's value by sharing it with others through the built-in catalog or on social media
    • Embed content within existing web pages or host it on your open data homepage
    • Enhance your data's reach with discovery, query, and metadata APIs
